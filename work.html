Tab_P Chrome Extension

This is an extension I made for Google Chrome. It runs a fuzzy search across your tab titles and URLs and gives you a list ordered by how well your search term matches each tab. I used qs_score.js to run the query. This is meant to help folks who sometimes find themselves with too many tabs open. You can browse the source on Github or add the extension via Chrome's Web Store:

Get the Extension

(PS - You can make it more useful by binding a keystroke to the extension in your Chrome settings)

Berrios Tax Cuts and Donors Map

As a researcher on Forrest Claypool's campaign for Cook Co Tax Assessor, I came into several decades of data on decisions our opponent, Joe Berrios, had made as to whose property taxes should be cut and whose should be maintained (Berrios sat on the three-person Cook Co Board of Review). We found that 94% of donors to Berrios's campaigns saw their property taxes reduced, and that a small number of corportate firms who were the biggest donors to Berrios were also the biggest recipents of Berrios's largesse on the Board of Review.

I made a map that shows which properties got tax cuts from Berrios. Each map marker pulls info on the attorney representing the property before Berrios, and how much this attorney or firm has donated to Berrios. The numbers show why the Better Gov't Association calls Berrios "pay-to-play personified."
