University of Chicago

AB in Fundamentals, minor in Classical Studies, Sep 2004 - Dec 2008

Deets: GPA 3.8/4.0, College Honors, Deans List, Journalism Program Fellow.

I often get asked "What is Fundamentals?" It's a Great Books-like program. Undergrads apply in their first year and, if selected, they spend the next three years reading canonical Western texts and taking classes with a focus on their "fundamental question." Mine was "What does identity have to do with ethics?" and I took classes ranging from Ulysses to Microeconomics in the course of this wild goose chase. I wrote my thesis on identity and recognition in Homer's Odyssey. I also studied abroad in Vienna and was an intern with a news agency in Sarajevo.
